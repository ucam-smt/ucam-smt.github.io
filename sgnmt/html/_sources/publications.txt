
Publications

 Felix Stahlberg, Eva Hasler, Aurelien Waite, and Bill Byrne. 
 Syntactically guided neural machine translation. 
 In *Proceedings of the 54th annual meeting of the Association for Computational Linguistics (ACL 16)*, August 2016. Berlin, Germany.
 `arXiv/1605.04569 <http://arxiv.org/abs/1605.04569>`_
